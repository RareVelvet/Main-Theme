Our Story

Beauty, kept beautifully.

A considered collection of beauty, skincare and elegant essentials — designed to make your everyday rituals feel a little more luxurious.

How it began

It started with the things we reach for every day.

Rare Velvet began with a simple frustration: the products we used daily rarely felt as considered as the rituals they were part of. A serum that worked but looked clinical. A jewelry box that stored, but didn't delight. Beautiful things, scattered across too many brands.

So we set out to build one place for the well-made and the everyday-luxurious — beauty, skincare and elegant essentials, each chosen for how it looks, feels and lasts. Curated by editors, not algorithms.

The name says it best. Rare, because every piece earns its place. Velvet, for the softness and care we bring to all of it.
